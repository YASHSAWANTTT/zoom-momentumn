CORE FEATURES

Four Engines of Engagement

Every feature designed to keep students focused, professors informed, and lectures alive.

Warm-Up Arena

AI-generated trivia from past lectures launches before class. A synchronized multiplayer game with a live leaderboard that primes students for what's ahead.

Live Anchor

A shared, auto-updating timeline of topics and takeaways generated from live transcription. Students always know what's being discussed and can bookmark key moments.

Professor's Pulse

One-tap AI-generated check-in polls that gauge comprehension in real time. Professors see results instantly and can adjust their teaching on the fly.

HOW IT WORKS

From Install to Impact in Minutes

No complex configuration. No learning curve. Just open Zoom and teach.

01

Install the Zoom App

Add Zoom Momentum from the Zoom Marketplace. It loads as an in-meeting side panel — zero setup for students.

02

Start Your Lecture

The Warm-Up Arena auto-launches trivia. Live Anchor begins building a shared timeline from the transcript stream.

03

Engage in Real Time

Professors trigger Pulse check-ins at any moment. Students bookmark confusion points. Everything syncs live.

04

Learn After Class

Recovery Agent generates personalized study packs from bookmarks. Students get targeted explanations and practice.
